Bibimbap

What is Bibambap

Ingredients

  • rice
  • eggs
  • carrots
  • mushrooms
  • cucumber
  • spinach
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • rice vinegar

How to Make

  1. prepare rice
  2. pickle carrots and cucumbers
  3. wilt spinach and marinate in soysauce / sesame oil
  4. sauté mushrooms and marinate in soysauce
  5. cook egg leaving the yoke runny
  6. add all ingredients to rice
  7. drizzle with sesame oil
recipe by Donald Maness
